# Fruity v1.0.0 — First Release 🍊

First public release of Fruity, a Progressive Web App for tracking daily fruit consumption.

## What's included

**Core app**
- 75 fruits with XP values, seasonal data, vitamin profiles, flavour tags, and storage guides
- 5 tabs: Today, Challenge, Knowledge, Passport, Me
- 15 XP levels from Seedling to King Durian, each unlocking a new feature

**Today tab**
- Log fruits daily with a searchable grid (Everyday fruits by default, all 75 in Show All mode)
- Vitamin coverage tracker across 6 vitamins
- Daily suggestions based on nutritional gaps
- Daily streak tracking

**Challenge tab**
- Monthly cooking challenges with 4 difficulty tiers (Easy 50 XP → Trio 250 XP)
- Fruit Roulette — weekly dare with +100 XP bonus
- Fruit Lab — weekly tea & ferment recipe
- Pairing of the Week — seasonal fruit pairings
- Friday Sips — weekly cocktail inspiration
- Fruit Bucket List

**Knowledge tab**
- Fruit Trivia — weekly rotating quiz
- Origins Quiz — geography & culture
- Fruit Keeper — storage guide for all 75 fruits

**Passport tab**
- 7 categories: Everyday, Berries, Pome & Stone, Citrus, Asian, Latin American, Rare Finds
- Multi-category support (fruits appear in all relevant sections)
- Category completion rewards: 300–1000 XP
- Achievements

**Me tab**
- Full history with daily Low / Good / Great scoring
- XP ladder with fruit icons per tier
- Flavour Wheel
- Pixel Art
- Yearly Stats
- Settings: Today grid mode, feedback link

**PWA**
- Fully offline after first load
- Installable on Android and iOS
- All 75 fruit icons embedded (no internet needed for images)
