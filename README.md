# 🍊 Fruity — Daily Fruit Tracker

> Track your daily fruit consumption, discover new varieties, and level up your fruit knowledge.

**[▶ Try it live →](https://maybeshiba.github.io/fruity-app/)**

---

## What is Fruity?

Fruity is a Progressive Web App (PWA) for tracking the fruit you eat every day. It's designed to make eating more fruit fun — through XP, levels, challenges, and discovery — rather than feeling like a chore.

Built as a single HTML file. No accounts. No servers. Your data stays on your device.

---

## Features

### 🍎 Today
Log every fruit you eat each day. See your vitamin coverage across 6 vitamins, get smart suggestions for what you're missing, and build a daily streak. Seasonal fruits earn double XP.

### 🍳 Challenge
Monthly cooking challenges with four difficulty tiers — Easy, Classic, Difficult, and Trio. Unlock Fruit Roulette, Pairing of the Week, Friday Sips, and the Fruit Bucket List as you level up.

### 🧠 Knowledge
Fruit Trivia, Origins Quiz, Fruit Lab (tea & ferment ideas), and Fruit Keeper (storage tips). Content rotates weekly and monthly.

### 🗺️ Passport
Stamp every new fruit you try across 7 categories: Everyday, Berries, Pome & Stone Fruits, Citrus, Asian Fruits, Latin American, and Rare Finds. 75 fruits to collect. Complete a category to claim bonus XP.

### 👤 Me
Your stats, full history, XP ladder, Flavour Wheel, Pixel Art, and Yearly Stats. 15 levels from 🌱 Seedling to 👑 King Durian, each unlocking a new feature.

---

## 75 Fruits

Covers everyday staples, seasonal German fruits, citrus varieties, Asian and Latin American imports, and 8 rare finds including Jackfruit, Feijoa, Ackee, Kiwano, and Salak.

## Install as PWA

Fruity works fully offline once installed.

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu → *Add to Home screen*
3. Tap *Add* — it will appear as an app icon

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button → *Add to Home Screen*
3. Tap *Add*

---

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step
- All fruit icons embedded as base64 (works fully offline)
- LocalStorage for all user data (no backend)
- Service Worker for offline caching
- Progressive Web App (installable, standalone display)
